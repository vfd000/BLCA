How to Join

Membership Donation

Annual membership donations help support our community initiatives:

  • Standard Membership: $50/year
  • Supporting Membership: $100/year
  • Patron Membership: $200/year
Payment Options

You can submit your membership donation through:

  • Online payment through our secure portal (coming soon)
  • Check payable to "Boulevard Lane Community Association"
  • Cash payments at monthly meetings
